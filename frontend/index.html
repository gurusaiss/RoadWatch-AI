About RoadWatch

Built for the National Road Safety Hackathon 2026 · CoERS, IIT Madras · Track: RoadWatch

RoadWatch addresses the accountability gap in India's road infrastructure — the disconnect between money sanctioned, money spent, and actual road quality. Citizens have no effective way to monitor contractor performance, verify public spending, or report defects to the right authority. RoadWatch closes this gap with AI.

🗺️

Road Data Accuracy

Every road has: road type, contractor name & contact, Executive Engineer, last relaying date, construction year, surface type — all with official source URLs.

💰

Budget Transparency

Sanctioned vs. spent per financial year with official government source links. Anomaly detection auto-flags under-utilised budgets below 65% — indicating possible delays or mismanagement.

📢

Smart Complaint Routing

4-step complaint wizard. AI + Authority database lookup routes every complaint to the exact Executive Engineer based on road type + state + country. Zero wrong departments.

📷

AI Damage Assessment

Upload a road photo — AI (Groq LLaMA 3.1 → GPT-4 → rule-based fallback) classifies damage type (pothole, crack, rutting…) and assigns a severity score 1–10.

🤖

AI Road Chatbot

Ask in natural language: "Who is the EE for NH-44?", "Show MDR-23 budget", "Which roads are critical in Tamil Nadu?" — instant AI answers backed by live database.

🌍

Global Coverage

13 countries: India (NHAI/PWD/PMGSY), UK, USA, Bangladesh, South Africa, Madagascar, Poland, Germany, Australia, Nigeria, Kenya, Japan, Malaysia — with local authorities.

📶

Offline / PWA

Service Worker caches all road data, stats, authorities. Works in low-connectivity rural areas. Installable as a mobile app from the browser.

📊

Analytics & Export

Live charts: condition distribution, avg score by country, complaint severity breakdown, budget utilisation by road type. CSV export for roads and complaints.
